Research Summary

My research focuses on perception-centered visual computing. I build explainable models that connect image features to human perceptual and affective responses. I‚Äôm especially interested in drawing on computational visual neuroscience to develop perceptually grounded metrics and objectives, with applications in compression and XR/VR.

  • Explainable representations: identifying controllable factors that drive preference and comfort
  • Human response modeling: modeling preference and comfort from image features
  • Neuro-behavioral grounding: linking features to perception via neural and psychophysical constraints
  • Perception-aware optimization: perceptual QoE objectives for immersive video coding and streaming
